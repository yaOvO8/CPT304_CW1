Privacy Policy

Overview

BizTrack is a front-end business tracking project. This site does not require a user account and does not send your product, order, or expense records to a back-end server.

What We Store

BizTrack stores data in your browser using local storage so the app can remember your records and preferences between visits.

  1. Products you add or edit.
  2. Orders you add or edit.
  3. Expenses you add or edit.
  4. Your cookie banner preference.

Why We Store It

The stored data is used only to keep the app functional on your device, including table contents, dashboard totals, charts, and the cookie banner choice.

Cookie Banner

The banner on this site lets you accept or dismiss the notice. You can change that choice at any time from the footer link on any page.

Third-Party Resources

BizTrack loads third-party front-end resources such as Font Awesome, Google Fonts, and ApexCharts CDNs. These services may receive standard browser requests when the page loads.

Your Control

You can clear BizTrack data at any time by clearing your browser storage for this site. Doing that removes saved records and resets the cookie banner preference.

Contact

If you want more context about the project, use the Meet the Developer page or the Help page.

BizTrack keeps data in your browser only.
