My Coding Journey

Hey, I'm Sumayyah!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where I'm rolling up my sleeves and diving into the coding world. I'm not your typical tech guru. I'm a small business owner navigating the hustle and bustle of entrepreneurship, and I'm also part of the GetCoding NL program.

BizTrack came out of the real challenge of managing products, orders, and finances while running a business. It's my practical answer to that daily chaos.

This project is my attempt to make life more straightforward for small business owners who are constantly multitasking and could use a break.

I'm a student in a software development program that is turning curiosity into real building skills, and BizTrack is where I put those lessons into practice.

A big part of that journey has been the guidance of my coach, Sam Russell, whose support helped make the path feel far less intimidating.

So this page is really an invitation. If you're curious about small business life, coding, or where those two worlds meet, you're welcome here.

Here's to coding, chaos, everything in between, and heartfelt thanks to Sam!
